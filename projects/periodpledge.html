Problem Statement

The Challenge of Period Poverty

Period poverty refers to the lack of access to menstrual products, hygiene facilities, education, and healthcare due to financial constraints or societal stigma. This issue disproportionately affects low-income individuals, students, and people in marginalized communities, leading to severe consequences such as poor menstrual hygiene, missed school or workdays, and long-term health risks.

In South Africa, research has shown that many schoolgirls miss up to a week of school per month due to lack of menstrual supplies, which contributes to gender inequality in education and career opportunities.

Proposed Solution

Comprehensive Mobile Platform

PeriodPledge addresses period poverty through a multi-faceted approach that combines:

  • Health Tracking: Comprehensive menstrual cycle monitoring and predictions
  • Education: Access to reliable reproductive health information
  • Community Support: Safe spaces for discussion and peer support
  • Resource Access: Direct connection to local NGOs and aid programs
  • Geographic Integration: Location-based services for finding nearby support

Target Audience & Market Reach

PeriodPledge primarily targets users in Africa, South Asia, Latin America, and Southeast Asia, where menstrual inequity is prevalent. The app focuses on:

Women and Girls (Ages 13-45)

Individuals who need reliable menstrual tracking and comprehensive health education resources

Students and Young Adults

Those facing period poverty in school and university settings who need support and resources

Low-Income Communities

Communities in rural and urban areas with limited access to menstrual health resources and products

Platform Choice: Android First

The application was developed exclusively for Android to effectively reach the primary users affected by period poverty. Key considerations included:

  • Affordability: Entry-level Android smartphones are significantly more affordable than iPhones in target markets
  • Market Share: Android holds 69.9% global market share, with even higher penetration in developing regions
  • Offline Support: Android's flexibility allows for applications optimized for offline functionality
  • Low-End Device Compatibility: Optimized for budget smartphones with minimal storage and battery usage
